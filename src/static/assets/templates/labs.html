What Are Labs?
 Labs are an interactive experience where you access remote environments and participate in coding exercises, development scenarios, and hands-on training in a remote desktop scenario. We offer two types of labs, depending on the course: External Labs:
If the lab session on this course is provided externally (from a 3rd party), you will be redirected to an external site. Credentials to access this lab (login and password) were sent to you in a separate email.

QuickStart Labs:
If the lab session on this course is a QuickStart lab, simply launch the course lab and begin. No login credentials are necessary.

 If you need assistance in accessing a lab or never received your lab credentials, please contact QuickStart support at (855) 800-8240.
